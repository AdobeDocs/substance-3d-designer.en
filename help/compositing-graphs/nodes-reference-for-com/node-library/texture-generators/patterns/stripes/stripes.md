## Description

Generates a tiling, angled, stripe pattern. The pattern adjusts itself to always ensure continuity.

## Parameters

* **Stripes**: *1 - 100*Sets the amount of stripes. Automatically shifts the result to ensure tiling.
* **Width**: *0.0 - 1.0*Sets Stripe width.
* **Softness**: *0.0 - 1.0*Sets the transition of the stripe edges.
* **Shift**: *0 - 20*Tilts the stripes. Automatically adds more stripes to ensure tiling.
* **Align**: *Edges, Center*Sets pivot for Shifting.
* **Filtering**: *False/True*Enables filtering.
* **Non Square Expansion**: *False/True*  
  Enables compensation of squash and stretch with non-square ratios.
