Substance 3D Designer is a material authoring software. It lets you use a node graph to generate textures from procedural patterns and noises, as well as manipulate bitmaps.

A word or concept used in Designer is unfamiliar to you? Go to our Glossary to learn about it.

If you have any question that is not answered in this manual, feel free to ask on our Community Support forum. You can also download our Physically Based Rendering guide if you wish to learn more about PBR.
